About me

Hello.

I’m Amelia O’Connor. I am a certified massage therapist, reiki practioner and spiritual guide.

I have always enjoyed helping people. While living in Japan, I became enamored with traditional Japanese culture and its inherent spirituality. Eventually, I discovered massage as a way to help people in need, and decided to get my certificate in 按摩 Anma, traditional Japanese Massage from the Shiatsu School in Tokyo.

I offer massage treatments, reiki healing and guided meditation sessions. All treatments are customized to your individual needs in order to create the most soothing and healing experience for you.
